Fall Into Fizz

Cinematic Storyboard — Fizz Soda "Dreamberry"
Duration 40–50 seconds
Format 16:9 Landscape
Tool Runway (Kling 3.0 Pro)
Deadline April 1, 2026
Creator ltandon
The Story
A burned-out young creative sits alone in his dimly lit studio, surrounded by clutter and the soft glow of pink LED light — he's checked out, empty, staring at nothing. He drops his head and takes a heavy breath. Then his eyes land on a Fizz Soda can sitting on his desk. The moment his fingers touch the can, a split-second flash of pink clouds rips through his mind. Confused, he looks at the can — and another flash hits, this time he's falling through a sunset sky. He commits, pops the can open, and takes a sip. His eyes glow pink. Suddenly cherry blossom petals explode around him, consuming the room, consuming him — and he's gone, free-falling through an endless dreamscape of pink and coral clouds, arms outstretched, pure bliss. Then — SNAP. A friend's fingers click next to his face. He blinks. He's back in the chair, in the dark room, holding a half-empty can of Fizz. He wasn't gone for a journey. He was gone for a sip.

Three-Way Structure

One sentence, three acts — the core framework
01
Setup — "The Weight"
0:00 – 0:12
Dark · Muted · Heavy
Establish the world: a burned-out creative alone in his studio, drained of color and energy. The room is dim, the monitor is off, the only life is a faint pink glow. Then he sees the can.
02
Midpoint — "The Touch"
0:12 – 0:25
Electric · Surreal · Rising
The tonal pivot. Cloud flashes hit on contact. He opens the can — THE POP breaks the world open. He sips. His eyes glow. The transformation begins. Reality starts dissolving.
03
Climax — "The Fall"
0:25 – 0:45
Euphoric · Dreamy · Snap
Cherry blossoms explode. The room disappears. He's falling through infinite pink clouds — pure bliss. Then a SNAP of fingers yanks him back to reality. He was gone for a sip.
Act 1
The Weight
Shot 01
4 sec
Close-Up — Exhaustion
The ad opens tight on his face. He's leaned back in the chair, eyes heavy and distant, staring at nothing. The chair drifts in a barely perceptible rotation. He blinks slowly once. A deep, exhausted exhale — his chest drops.
CameraStatic close-up, slight low angle. Minimal movement.
AudioRoom ambience. The deep exhale is prominent. Faint hum.
ColorDesaturated, cool tones. Pink LED glow on left side of face.
Shot 02
4 sec
Wide Shot — Head Drop & Heavy Breath
We pull back to reveal the full room — the cluttered creative workspace, the dark monitor, the shelves of abandoned projects. He's a small figure in a space that used to inspire him. His head drops forward, shoulders sink. One heavy breath.
CameraWide shot, low angle. Chair drifts in slow lazy rotation.
AudioHeavy breath. Chair creak. Silence pressing in.
NoteThe Fizz Soda can is visible on the desk — but the viewer doesn't notice it yet.
Shot 03
4 sec
Can Close-Up — He Reaches
Close-up at desk level. The Fizz Soda can sits in sharp focus — condensation catching the pink light — while everything else falls into soft bokeh. A hand slowly enters frame from the right, fingers wrapping around the cold can, lifting it off the surface.
CameraLow angle from desk level. Shallow depth of field. Product hero shot.
AudioA faint low hum begins — something is about to change.
ProductFirst clear product feature. "FIZZ dreamberry" label fully visible.
↓ the moment everything shifts ↓
Act 2
The Touch
Flash Cut — First Glimpse of Clouds
The instant his fingers touch the can — a split-second rip. He's falling through massive pink cotton-candy clouds. A sunset sky. Gone before you can process it.
0.3–0.5 sec — WHOOSH sound
Shot 04
2 sec
Reaction — "What Just Happened?"
Back to reality. He's shaken. Hand comes up to his face — rubbing his eyes, touching his forehead. He looks at the can in disbelief. What was that? The room feels smaller, duller than before.
CameraMedium close-up. Same muted room.
AudioSilence. A heartbeat begins — faint, building.
Flash Cut — Falling Silhouette
Another flash — longer this time. His silhouette floats against a vivid sunset, arms outstretched. The pull is getting stronger. He can't resist.
0.3–0.5 sec — WHOOSH (longer)
POP
The tonal pivot of the entire ad
Shot 05
3 sec
The Pop — He Opens the Can
He commits. Extreme close-up on his thumb pressing the tab. Slow-motion feel. Condensation mist releases. THE POP — crisp, satisfying, world-cracking. A beat of pure silence after. This is the radio dispatch of this film. Everything after this is different.
CameraExtreme close-up on can tab. Slow-motion energy.
AudioTHE POP. Exaggerated, crisp. Then silence.
PivotThis sound breaks the ad in half. Before = weight. After = flight.
Shot 06
3 sec
The Sip — Eye Glow
He takes the first sip. A beat of stillness. Then — extreme close-up on his eye. The iris transforms, radiating a vivid pink-magenta glow outward from the pupil. A gentle breeze moves his hair even though he's indoors. The dream is taking hold.
CameraMedium → rapid push-in to extreme macro on eye.
AudioRising ethereal tone. Breeze. Dream soundtrack begins.
ColorRoom desaturation lifts. Pink glow blooms from the iris.
↓ reality dissolves ↓
Act 3
The Fall
Shot 07
3 sec
Blossom Explosion
Pink cherry blossom petals erupt around him — not falling gently but BURSTING outward. The room itself begins dissolving into petals. His physical form starts to blur and merge with the blossoms. The real world is being consumed.
CameraPull back to show full explosion. Room still partially visible.
AudioFull euphoric dream soundtrack. Lush, warm, building.
Text"Fall in love again & again" appears in soft pink typography.
Shot 08
2 sec
Consumed — Bliss Close-Up
Tight on his face. Eyes closed. Peaceful. Petals swirl everywhere — in his hair, across his cheeks. The room is almost completely gone. He's surrendered to it. Pure sensory bliss.
CameraClose-up, soft focus. Dreamlike quality.
AudioMusic building. Wind through leaves. Gentle chimes.
Shot 09
4 sec
The Fall — Cloud Dreamscape
He's fully gone. The room has vanished. He floats through massive pink and coral clouds in a sunset sky — same dark coat, arms outstretched, tumbling gently. This is the taste of Fizz Soda visualized as pure, unbounded freedom.
CameraWide shot → tracking shot orbiting around him as he falls.
AudioFull euphoria. Wind rushing. Faint fizzing/carbonation woven in.
PeakThe most visually spectacular moment of the ad.
↓ SNAP — back to reality ↓
Shot 10
3 sec
The Snap — Back to Reality
HARD CUT. The room. The chair. A hand enters from the right — SNAP — fingers click next to his face. His eyes refocus. He blinks. The dreamy expression fades. He's slightly confused, slightly disappointed to be back. Like waking from the best dream of your life.
CameraSame angle as Shot 1. Close-up. Hand enters from off-screen.
AudioSNAP cuts all music dead. Room tone returns. Silence.
PayoffThe recontextualization — he wasn't on a journey, he was drinking a soda.
